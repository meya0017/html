Nelson Mandela

Basic Information

  • Full Name: Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela
  • Born: July 18, 1918
  • Died: December 5, 2013
  • Nationality: South African
  • Occupation: Anti-apartheid revolutionary, political leader, philanthropist

Major Achievements

  1. First democratically elected President of South Africa (1994–1999)
  2. Leader in the anti-apartheid movement
  3. Nobel Peace Prize laureate (1993)
  4. Established the Truth and Reconciliation Commission
  5. Promoted peace, equality, and human rights worldwide
